History of Our Bakery

Pink Boxe has a rich history that dates back to 1988. It all started when Dani, a passionate baker with a vision, opened the doors to our first bakery in L.A. From humble beginnings, we embarked on a journey to delight our customers with the finest baked goods and exceptional service.

Over the years, we have remained dedicated to our craft, using only the highest quality ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our commitment to excellence has earned us the trust and loyalty of our community, making us a beloved destination for all things sweet and savory.

Through ups and downs, we have persevered, adapting to changing tastes and trends while staying true to our roots. Today, our bakery stands as a testament to hard work, passion, and the belief that good food has the power to bring people together.

As we look to the future, we remain committed to providing our customers with unforgettable culinary experiences and continuing the legacy of Dani. Join us on this journey as we continue to bake memories and create moments of joy for generations to come.
